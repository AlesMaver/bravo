About {{ config['BROWSER_NAME'] }}

This variant browser currently shows chromosome locations, alleles, VEP functional annotations, and allele frequencies for {{ "{:,}".format(num_variants) }} variants observed in {{ "{:,}".format(config['NUM_SAMPLES']) }} individuals sequenced in NHLBI's TOPMed program.

The information is shared by TOPMed Phase 1 and Phase 2 studies and is available for users that agree to the terms.

The sequencing analysis pipeline consists of two major processes:

  1. Harmonization of data from the BAM files provided by the sequencing centers and
  2. Joint variant discovery and genotype calling across studies.
The alignment pipeline is available at github.com/statgen/gotcloud and the variant calling pipeline is available at github.com/statgen/topmed_freeze3_calling.

If you have a question about this website, contact pjvh@umich.edu.
